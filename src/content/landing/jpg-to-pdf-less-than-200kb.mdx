Converting JPG photos to a PDF under 200 KB is the most commonly needed conversion size for Indian government portal uploads. The 200 KB limit appears across JoSAA (for IIT/NIT admissions), state-level college counselling portals, SSC document portals, and several Aadhaar-related services.

At 200 KB, a single-page document PDF has enough room for good image quality — approximately 150 DPI effective resolution for standard letter or A4 documents. This is the sweet spot between file size and legibility: small enough for most government portals, clear enough for document verification staff to read all required fields.
