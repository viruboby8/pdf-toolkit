Converting JPG photos to a PDF under 500 KB covers one of the most versatile size ranges for Indian government and financial portals. At 500 KB, you have enough budget for 2–3 document pages at good resolution, making this target ideal for multi-page documents like Aadhaar card (front and back), PAN plus address proof, or a short bank statement.

The 500 KB limit appears in:

- **CKYC portal** — mandatory cap for stockbroker and mutual fund KYC uploads
- **NSDL PAN card portal** — address proof must be under 300 KB; 500 KB provides margin
- **myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in** — accepts up to 2 MB, but 500 KB is recommended for reliability
- **MHT-CET ARO** (Maharashtra engineering admissions) — 500 KB per document
- **VFS Global application portals** — per-document limits often 500 KB to 2 MB
- **Insurance KYC portals** — IRDA guidelines result in 500 KB caps on many insurer platforms

The 500 KB range is also where multi-page documents start becoming practical.
